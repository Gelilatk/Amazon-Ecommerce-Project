# Amazon Ecommerce Product Analysis

## Project Overview
This project analyzes Amazon product data to identify key drivers of product popularity and customer satisfaction. The analysis focuses on understanding how pricing, customer engagement, ratings, and product characteristics influence product performance.

The dataset contains **1,465 Amazon product records** and includes information such as product prices, ratings, reviews, and categories.

---

## Project Objectives
The main objective of this project is to identify the key factors that influence product success on Amazon. The analysis focuses on:

- Pricing strategy
- Customer engagement
- Customer satisfaction
- Product performance

---

## Data Analysis Process
The following steps were performed in this project:

1. Cleaned and prepared the Amazon product dataset
2. Performed Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
3. Created visualizations to identify patterns and trends
4. Generated business insights based on the data

The analysis was performed using **Python**, with libraries such as **Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, and Seaborn**.

---

## Key Insights
- Product success is primarily **demand-driven rather than discount-driven**.
- Customer satisfaction is generally **high across products**.
- Product popularity depends on **multiple factors beyond price and ratings**.
- Strategic pricing and product relevance play a major role in growth.

---

## Business Recommendations
Based on the analysis, the following recommendations were made:

- Focus on **product relevance rather than heavy discounting**
- Leverage **high-performing products** to increase sales
- Optimize **category-level strategy**
- Align product offerings with **customer demand**
- Use **data-driven monitoring** to track product performance
